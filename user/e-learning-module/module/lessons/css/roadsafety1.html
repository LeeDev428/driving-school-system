Road Safety Rules

The set of guidelines and regulations designed to ensure the safety of drivers, passengers, pedestrians, and other road users


Safety Guides



  • Obey All Traffic Signals and Signs

    Follow stoplights, road markings, and LTO-approved traffic signs.



    Tagalog: Sundin ang mga stoplight, road markings, at LTO-approved traffic signs.

    • Don’t Drink and Drive

      Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs is punishable under RA 10586.



      Tagalog: Ang pagmamaneho sa ilalim ng impluwensya ng alak o droga ay parurusahan sa ilalim ng RA 10586.

      • Avoid Distracted Driving

        Texting, calling, or using gadgets while driving is prohibited under RA 10913 (Anti-Distracted Driving Act).



        Tagalog: Ang pagte-text, pagtawag, o paggamit ng gadgets habang nagmamaneho ay ipinagbabawal sa ilalim ng RA 10913 (Anti-Distracted Driving Act).

        • Follow Speed Limits

          As mandated by RA 4136, speeding is a top cause of accidents. Always check speed limit signs.



          Tagalog: Ayon sa mandato ng RA 4136, ang overspeeding ay isa sa mga pangunahing sanhi ng aksidente. Laging tingnan ang mga speed limit signs.

          • Use Headlights Properly

            Switch on headlights from sunset to sunrise or during bad weather. Always dim high beams when another vehicle is approaching.



            Tagalog: Buksan ang headlights mula sa paglubog ng araw hanggang sa pagsikat ng araw o sa panahon ng masamang panahon. Laging i-dim ang high beams kapag may paparating na ibang sasakyan.

            • Drive Safely in Bad Weather

              Reduce speed, keep distance, and turn on headlights and wipers.



              Tagalog: Bawasan ang bilis, panatilihin ang distansya, at buksan ang headlights at wipers.

              • Overtake Only When Safe

                Overtaking is not allowed at intersections, pedestrian lanes, bridges, blind curves, or when road markings prohibit it.



                Tagalog: Ang pag-overtake ay hindi pinapayagan sa mga intersection, pedestrian lanes, bridges, blind curves, o kapag ipinagbabawal ito ng mga road markings.

                • Give Way to Pedestrians

                  Pedestrians have right of way at pedestrian crossings (marked or unmarked).



                  Tagalog: Ang mga pedestrian ay may karapatan sa pedestrian crossings (marked o unmarked).

                  • Maintain Safe Distance (2-Second Rule)

                    Keep at least a 2-second gap between you and the car in front (longer if raining or slippery).



                    Tagalog: Panatilihin ang hindi bababa sa 2-segundong agwat sa pagitan mo at ng kotse sa harap (mas mahaba kung umuulan o madulas).

                    • Check Vehicle Before Driving (BLOWBAGETS)

                      Brakes, Lights, Oil, Water, Battery, Air, Gas, Engine, Tires, Self.



                      Tagalog: Preno, Ilaw, Langis, Tubig, Baterya, Hangin, Gas, Makina, Gulong, Sarili.
